National Parks

How much do you know about national parks? Test your knowledge with some fun questions! Reveal answers.

Where was the world's first national park?
Right here! Yellowstone, created in 1872 by President Ulysses S. Grant, was the world's first. Its caretakers? the cavalry.
Which national park is the largest?
Wrangell, located in south central Alaska. It was designated on December 1, 1978 by President Jimmy Carter and is larger than the nation of Switzerland!
Where's the nation's deepest cave system?
In New Mexico, Carlsbad Caverns National Park is home to the deepest cave in the US, which comes in at 1,593 feet deep.
What's the largest volcano on earth?
Mauna Loa Volcano in Hawaii Volcanoes National Park is the largest volcano on earth. It rises more than 50,000 feet above its base, including a portion which is beneath the ocean.
Where is the newest U.S. national park?
Pinnacles National Park, located in California was designated on January 10, 2013, making it America's newest national park.
Which state has the most national parks in its borders?
It's a tie - Alaska and California both take the title, with 8 parks each.

How old are the trees at Redwoods National Park in California?
The average age of a redwood is 500-700 years old, but some can live to be 2,000 years old!
